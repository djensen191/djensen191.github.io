About Me

Hi! My name is Daniel Jensen. I am a Controls Engineer and technologist in Estacada, Oregon.

Welcome! My name is Daniel Jensen. I am a graduate from Oregon State University, with a Bachelor's of Science in Electrical and Computer Engineering and a Minor in Computer Science. Now I work for Toyo Tanso USA managing a network of Programmable Logic Controllers as well as managing a variety of data analysis tasks.

As a student at Oregon State I fell in love with software development, especially Python, C, & C++. My software development experience ranges from web development to application development. I enjoyed studying the inner workings of operating systems, memory management, file structures and multimedia compression. (Click here to visit my page for the different languages I have worked with.)

My senior design project at Oregon State University was to design and build a Smart Power Strip with a team of two other people. I served as the web designer for the project and chose to use a Raspberry Pi micro-controller. I learned how to use the Python Flask development framework, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build a dynamic website.

I joined the military after graduating high school and have served in both the Oregon Army National Guard and the Oregon Air National Guard. I have served as a guardsman for a total of 13 years. In this time I have deployed to both to Iraq and to the United Arab Emirates. These experiences have strengthened my tenacity to succeed and taught me many valuable leadership skills. I have also had unique opportunities in the Air National Guard to apply my engineering knowledge to solve technical problems.
